Teaching Method & Philosophy

1. Customized Learning Tailored to Your Level

Drawing on my extensive background as a former secondary school teacher, I break down complex Korean grammar and structures into easy-to-understand, systematic steps. I guide you progressively from foundational concepts to practical applications.

2. Mastery of Particles & Subtle Nuances

I help you clearly master Korean-specific particles and subtle nuances that are often difficult to grasp through self-study. Having lived in the UK for many years, I can provide flawless, smooth communication and explanations in English.

3. Real, Living Korean Beyond Textbooks

Instead of sticking purely to rigid, textbook expressions, my lessons focus on dynamic conversational and trendy business expressions that you can immediately use in daily life or professional Korean workplaces.
