About

Hi, I am Milim Kim, a professional and certified Korean language tutor based in Liverpool, UK, where I live with my husband and two daughters. Balancing my family life with my deep passion for teaching, I am dedicated to helping students of all levels achieve fluency in the Korean language.

Before relocating to the UK in 2012, I spent 8 years working as a licensed English teacher in public middle schools in South Korea after passing the rigorous national employment exam. This background provided me with a deep, systematic understanding of language acquisition and classroom management. Since May 2017, I have successfully taught over 100 students from diverse cultural backgrounds, ranging from secondary school students to full-time professionals, offering both tailored 1-to-1 lessons and engaging group sessions.

My teaching methodology focuses on practical communication, interactive multi-media resources, and customised curricula designed around each student's unique goals—whether it is conversational fluency, business proficiency, or TOPIK preparation. I hold a Certificate of Korean Language Teacher (Level 2) issued by the Korean Government, ensuring the highest standards of professional instruction.
