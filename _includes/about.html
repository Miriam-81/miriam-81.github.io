About

Hello, I am Milim Kim. I hold the Certificate of Korean Language Teacher (Level 2) from the National Institute of Korean Language (Korea, 2020) and have over five years of experience teaching Korean as a private tutor, working with more than 50 students.

Before moving to the UK in 2012, I worked for over eight years as an English teacher at middle schools in Korea after passing the Gyeonggi-do Secondary Teacher Employment Examination. I also studied Education and Second Language Teaching at university. This background helps me support students in learning Korean effectively and improving their communication skills with confidence.

I have taught a wide range of learners from diverse cultural backgrounds, including secondary school students and full-time professionals, through tailored one-to-one lessons and engaging group classes.

My lessons range from beginner to advanced levels and include learner-motivation-centred curricula as well as systematic preparation for TOPIK I and TOPIK II.

I look forward to continuing to provide meaningful and fruitful learning experiences for students in the future.
